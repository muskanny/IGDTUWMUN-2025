About IGDTUWMUN'25


The IGDTUW Model United Nations is more than just a conference—it’s a transformative experience that combines intellect, passion, and purpose. Hosted by Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women, the only women’s engineering university in India, IGDTUW MUN stands as a beacon of innovation, empowerment, and excellence.As you step into this forum of diplomacy and debate, you’ll find yourself amidst some of the brightest minds, engaging in meaningful discussions and crafting innovative solutions to global issues. With well-researched committees and thought-provoking agendas, every delegate is challenged to think critically, articulate confidently, and negotiate effectively. This is where you grow, not just as a speaker, but as a visionary leader who understands the complexities of the world.
